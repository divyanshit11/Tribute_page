Tribute to Iron Man

Iron Man, portrayed by Robert Downey Jr., is a fictional superhero appearing in the Marvel Comics and the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). Created by writer and editor Stan Lee, along with scripter Larry Lieber and artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby, Iron Man made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense #39 in March 1963.

Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist, genius inventor, and playboy, is the alter ego of Iron Man. With his advanced armored suit equipped with various technological capabilities, he fights against evil and protects the world as Iron Man. He is known for his wit, intelligence, and charisma, becoming one of the most beloved characters in the MCU.

Iron Man played a pivotal role in the formation of the Avengers and has appeared in multiple MCU films, including the Iron Man trilogy, The Avengers, and Avengers: Endgame, leaving a lasting impact on the franchise and the hearts of fans worldwide.
